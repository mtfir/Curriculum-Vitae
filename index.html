About Me

My name is Muchammad Tegar Firmansyah. I am a sophomore Electrical Engineering undergraduate student at Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology. I described myself as an introvert that sometime feel comfortable with his own bussiness but on the other time feel empty inside. I have very high interest in Science and Technology as it was my main reason to choose my major. I often watch anime and play guitar on my spare time. As an engineering student, I have basic knowledge in PCB Design. I also can program basic C, C++ and Arduino. Currently, I am interested in Digital Signal processing and Analog Circuit Design. I planned to take concentration in Electronics or RF engineering as it is the the most rewarding and in demand field right now.

My Journey

I was born in Tuban on 15 September 2002. I started Elementary Education in Latsari Elementary School Tuban. In this place, I grew my interest in science through reading some science book and great scientist life story avaliable in city library. One of my teacher saw that spark of interest on me and offered me and my 2 friends to join science olympiad extracurricular and I was gladly accepted it. I joined city level science olympiad and got second place. I was very glad that time because it was the first time I've ever got a trophy. Then I continued to Junior High School 3 Tuban. Here I've achieved my highest achievement in my life in the field of Science. I joined science olympiad extracurricular too in here. In the first year, I got second place in city level science olympiad but can't make it to province level but in the second year I can make it to province level. It is very unfortunate that I cannot make it to nation level but that moment still got a special place in my heart along with my first victory in Elementary School. Then I went to Senior High School 1 Tuban. Here I decided to join physics olympiad extracurricular because physics seems more challenging than the other fields at that time. Here I can only make it to city level physics olympiad in the second year so I felt a bit frustrated that time. But aside that, I found a really nice friends who has the same hobby here.
